Local News Research Resource (LNRR)

We're building a searchable database of academic research on local news to help policymakers, advocates, and journalists access evidence supporting local journalism initiatives.

Why This Matters

Decades of academic research demonstrate the civic importance of local journalism, but this evidence is often scattered across journals and paywalls. Our resource makes this research accessible to those working to strengthen local news.

What's Included

The resource catalogs peer-reviewed research examining local news effects across multiple dimensions:

  • Political participation and civic engagement
  • Government accountability and transparency
  • Community knowledge and information quality
  • Democratic norms and polarization
  • Economic and social outcomes

Status

Currently in development. The resource will launch with comprehensive coverage of research on local news effects, with ongoing updates as new research is published.
